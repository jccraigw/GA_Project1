Deep Sea Treasure Hunt

Rules/Instructions:

  1. Hunt for treasure boxes! 1pt per box.
  2. Avoid Sharks! Each bite takes a point off your life.
  3. Don't get stuck between the coral and the top.
  4. Touching the bottom will cause you to bounce up.
  5. Sharks will become enraged and move faster the longer you live!
  6. Press restart to play again after game is over.
